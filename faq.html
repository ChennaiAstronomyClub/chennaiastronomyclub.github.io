Is there any membership fee or age restriction?

No. There is no registration fee or age restriction involved. However, people below 18 should bring a consent letter signed by their parent for any event that involves travelling out of the city.

Do you have an office?

We are primarily based out of Facebook even though we meet in various locations.

I am new to astronomy. Where can I buy telescopes? (and/or)
Which telescope to buy?

If you are just getting started, DO NOT BUY A TELESCOPE.
Get started with naked eye observations or binoculars and learn the basics of astronomy and visual observations first. Get a telescope only after 2 years of doing this. By then, you would have already figured out which telescope to buy.

Will you come to our house and train our kid on using a telescope?

No.
